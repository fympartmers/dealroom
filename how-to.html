How to Access & Review Deals

01

Browse Available Deals

Navigate through our curated selection of investment opportunities on the main Deals page. Each deal card provides high-level information including sector, deal size, and target returns. Take note of deals marked as "Active" for immediate opportunities or "Upcoming" for pipeline deals.

02

Request Access via NDA

Click "View Deal" on any opportunity that interests you. You'll be prompted to complete a Non-Disclosure Agreement form. This protects sensitive information and ensures all parties maintain confidentiality. Submit your details including your organization and role for verification.

03

Receive Deal Materials

Once your NDA is reviewed and approved (typically within 24 hours), you'll receive access to comprehensive deal materials via secure link. This includes detailed financial models, due diligence reports, management presentations, and transaction structure details.

04

Initial Review & Analysis

Conduct your preliminary assessment of the opportunity. Review key metrics, growth projections, risk factors, and strategic fit with your investment thesis. Our team is available to answer initial questions and provide additional context as needed.

05

Express Interest

If the opportunity aligns with your investment criteria, submit a formal expression of interest through our Contact form or directly to your FYM Partners relationship manager. Include your preliminary investment range and any specific areas requiring further diligence.

06

Deep Dive & Due Diligence

Qualified investors proceed to comprehensive due diligence. This includes management meetings, site visits, expert calls, and access to the virtual data room. Our team facilitates the process and coordinates with all stakeholders to ensure smooth execution.

Important Considerations

  • Confidentiality: All information shared is strictly confidential and subject to the signed NDA. Do not share deal details with external parties without explicit permission.
  • Timelines: Most active deals have defined timelines for expressions of interest and final bids. Early engagement is encouraged to allow sufficient time for evaluation.
  • Minimum Tickets: Each opportunity has minimum investment thresholds. These are typically outlined in the initial deal summary but can be discussed for strategic investors.
  • Co-investment: Many deals offer co-investment opportunities alongside lead investors. Indicate your preference for lead or co-investment positions when expressing interest.
  • Support: Our team provides full transaction support including structuring advice, local market insights, and coordination with legal and financial advisors.

Need Assistance?

Our deal team is ready to support your investment journey. For questions about specific opportunities or the investment process, please reach out.
